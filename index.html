We don't build replacements. We build continuations — augmentations that read your nervous system's own intent and finish the sentence it started, faster and further than tissue alone ever could.

Augmentations

Four systems, one integration

Each augmentation is fitted individually and calibrated to your own neural signature over a two-week window.

Neural Lace

A mesh interface layered across the motor cortex, translating intention into signal before the thought finishes forming.

Ocular Array

Replacement optics with adjustable spectral range, low-light gain, and a heads-up telemetry layer you can switch off.

Dermal Plating

Sub-surface panels that harden on impact and stay pliable everywhere else, worn instead of felt.

Reflex Spine

A parallel signal path along the spinal column that shortens reaction time without overriding voluntary control.

Process

Four appointments, in order

This sequence doesn't compress — each stage depends on data from the one before it.

01

Consultation

We map what you want extended and rule out what shouldn't be.

02

Mapping

Forty-eight hours of passive signal capture to build your neural baseline.

03

Integration

Fitting is done in a single procedure, under local sedation where possible.

04

Calibration

Two weeks of tuning sessions until the augmentation stops feeling installed and starts feeling used.
